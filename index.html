Ismailia

Nemra 6

Nemra 6 Gardens are considered one of the most famous gardens in Ismailia Governorate, because they contain rare trees and plants. They are a source of attraction for citizens to spend special times, especially after the reforms that the streets and squares of the governorate witnessed during the last period. Number 6 Gardens are located along Muhammad Ali Street and up to Number 6 Ferry, overlooking the Suez Canal.

Suez Canal

The Suez Canal is an artificial sea-level waterway in Egypt, connecting the Mediterranean Sea to the Red Sea. It is a popular trade route between Europe and Asia, but also a source of conflict and controversy,
Ismailia is one of three cities overlooking the canal

Ismaily SC

Ismaily Sporting Club is an Egyptian professional football club, established on 20 March 1921 as El Nahda Sporting Club based in Ismaïlia, Egypt. The club is best known for its football team. Also it's considered as an Egyptian National Club, where they helped the famous local tour of duty to play for the benefit of the homeland against the occupying armies. The club's nickname The Brazilians, is a reference to their uniforms, which echo those of the Brazil national team, and similar style of play.
